About StageDoor

StageDoor is a searchable and filterable catalog of musicals, categorized by genre, tone, musical style, and narrative structure.

Each show features a spoiler-free overview, full story synopsis, song-by-song timeline, cast listings, and “Also” recommendations — everything you need in one place.
